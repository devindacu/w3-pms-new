🏨 W3 Hotel PMS — Overview

W3 Hotel PMS is a comprehensive, cloud-hosted Property Management System designed for hotels of all sizes. It covers the full guest journey — from online booking through check-in, stay operations, housekeeping, food & beverage, procurement, finance, and check-out — with AI-powered insights layered on top.

🖥 Front Office
Reservations, check-in/out, folios, guest invoices.
🧹 Housekeeping
Room status, task assignment, linen tracking.
🍽 F&B / POS
Full restaurant POS, menu management, kitchen orders.
📊 Finance
Accounts payable/receivable, GL journal, reconciliation.
🛒 Procurement
Purchase orders, GRN, invoice matching, supplier management.
🤖 AI Hub
Demand forecasting, auto review replies, upsell engine.
📡 Channel Manager
Sync rates & availability with OTAs in real time.
👥 CRM
Guest profiles, loyalty, complaints, review management.

🚀 Quick Start

First Login

  1. Navigate to the PMS URL provided by your administrator.
  2. Enter your username and temporary password.
  3. If prompted, set a new personal password (8+ characters, mixed case + number).
  4. The system lands on the Dashboard.

Initial Configuration Wizard

First-time setup is guided by the Configuration Wizard (Settings → Setup Wizard). It walks you through:

  • Hotel Profile — name, address, contact, logo, branding colours.
  • Room Types & Rooms — define types (Deluxe, Suite…), then individual rooms.
  • Rate Plans — base rack rate, season-based and promotional rates.
  • Tax & Service Charge — VAT, tourism levy, service charge %.
  • User Accounts — create staff logins with appropriate roles.
💡 Tip
Complete the wizard before taking reservations to ensure all pricing and tax rules are active.

Navigation

The left sidebar groups modules by category. On mobile the sidebar collapses; tap the ☰ icon to open it. Use the global search bar (top of main area) to jump directly to a guest, reservation, or room.

🛎 Front Office

The Front Office module is the heart of the PMS, handling the complete guest journey.

Reservation Management

  • Create, edit, and cancel reservations with date pickers.
  • Assign specific rooms or let the system auto-assign.
  • Support for Group Reservations — one master booking for multiple guests.
  • Availability Calendar — colour-coded grid view of all rooms.
  • Attach Rate Plans (BAR, Package, Corporate, OTA).
  • Capture deposits and pre-payments.

Check-In Process

  1. Search for the guest's reservation (by name, ID, or booking number).
  2. Verify ID and confirm room assignment.
  3. Click Check In — room status changes to Occupied.
  4. Generate and print a Registration Card.

Folio Management

Every reservation has a Folio — a running account of all charges:

  • Room charges (auto-posted nightly).
  • F&B charges posted from POS.
  • Extra services (spa, laundry, minibar).
  • Manual charge posting.
  • Split folio between multiple payers.
  • Adjustments, discounts, and voids.

Check-Out & Guest Invoice

  1. Review the folio for completeness.
  2. Apply any discounts or adjustments.
  3. Process payment (cash, card, city-ledger, multiple methods).
  4. Generate Guest Invoice (A4 PDF, email, or print).
  5. Room status changes to Dirty → triggers Housekeeping task.

Guest Invoice Features

  • Multiple invoice types: Guest Folio, Room-Only, F&B-Only, Proforma, Credit Note, Debit Note.
  • Tax breakdowns (VAT, Tourism Levy) auto-calculated.
  • Multi-currency support with live exchange rates.
  • Company/corporate billing with GST number.
  • Digital signature capture.

🗺 Floor Plan

The Floor Plan view provides a visual, drag-and-drop hotel map with live room status overlays.

  • Build multi-floor layouts with rooms, corridors, and amenities.
  • Colour-coded room states: Clean ✅, Dirty 🟡, Occupied 🔵, Out of Order 🔴, Inspected ✨.
  • Click any room to open its detail — current guest, folio balance, housekeeping notes.
  • Drag-and-drop to reassign guest to a different room (with folio transfer).

🧹 Housekeeping

Room Status Workflow

Room status flows: Occupied → Departure → Dirty → Cleaning → Inspected → Clean → Occupied.

Task Assignment

  • Auto-generate daily task list based on departures and stays.
  • Assign rooms to specific housekeepers by floor or section.
  • Set priority (Urgent, High, Normal).
  • Mobile-friendly task view for staff using phones/tablets.

Amenities & Linen Tracking

  • Track minibar consumption per room per stay.
  • Record linen usage and losses.
  • Low-stock alerts trigger procurement requests.

Lost & Found

Log found items with description, location, date, and staff name. Link to guest for return.

Batch Operations

Select multiple rooms and update status, assign housekeeper, or mark inspected in one action.

🌙 Night Audit

Night Audit closes the business day and posts room charges to all in-house folios.

  1. Verify all check-ins and check-outs are complete.
  2. Review the Audit Checklist — flags any discrepancies.
  3. Run Room Charge Posting — posts rate × nights to every occupied folio.
  4. Generate the Daily Report — occupancy, revenue, departures for next day.
  5. Confirm audit to advance the system date.
⚠ Important
Night audit cannot be undone. Ensure all transactions are finalised before confirming.

❓ Help & FAQ

Common Questions

Q: How do I reset a user's password?

Go to Settings → User Management, find the user, click Edit, and use the Reset Password button. A temporary password is generated and emailed to the user, who must change it on next login.

Q: A room is stuck in "Dirty" status — how do I fix it?

In Housekeeping, find the room, click the status badge, and manually update it to Clean or Inspected. You can also do this from the Floor Plan.

Q: How do I apply a discount to a guest's folio?

Open the folio from Front Office, click on the line item, and choose Adjust. Enter the discount amount or percentage and save. The adjustment posts as a negative charge.

Q: Channel Manager is not syncing — what do I check?

Navigate to Channel Manager → Connections. Check the last-sync timestamp and connection status. Click Test Connection. If red, verify the API credentials in the OTA extranet have not expired.

Q: How do I void a posted journal entry?

In Finance → Journal Entries, find the entry and click Reverse. A new reversing entry is posted for the same amounts with opposite signs and today's date.

Q: Why is the Night Audit blocked?

The audit checklist highlights open items. Common blockers: unchecked-out departures, unapproved folios, or posted charges with no payment. Resolve each flagged item and re-run the audit.

Q: How do I enable the AI features?

Go to Settings → AI Configuration, select your provider (OpenAI or Gemini), paste your API key, and toggle the features you want. Save settings. AI features appear in the AI Hub sidebar module.

Q: Can I export all my hotel data?

Yes. Go to Settings → Backup & Data and click Export Full Data. This generates a JSON archive of all records. Use the Rollback Wizard to restore from a previous backup.

Q: How are OTA reservations mapped to rooms?

During Channel Manager setup, you map each OTA room category to a PMS room type. When a booking arrives, the PMS assigns an available room of that type. You can change the specific room assignment in Front Office before check-in.

Q: What does RevPAR mean and where is it shown?

Revenue Per Available Room = ADR × Occupancy %. It is displayed on the Revenue Management dashboard and in the Analytics module under Revenue KPIs.
